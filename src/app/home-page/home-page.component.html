About Me

Ayman is an eager and passionate Computer Engineer. He recieved his degree from UC Irvine in Computer Engineering in 2023 and is constantly looking to improve his skillset. He is currently a Software Associate at Charles Schwab. During his time at UCI, he was a Hardware Engineering Intern for ButterflyMX, an access control system and SaaS company. There he researched RFID and BLE access control for home and office use as well as tested and manufactured network conected elevator controllers. His dedication to learning has earned him a reputation among his colleagues as someone who is always eager to take on new challenges and solve complex problems. Outside of work, Ayman loves to engage in personal projects, tinkering with electronic gadgets, and coding innovative applications in his spare time. He believes that personal projects are an excellent way to further develop his skills and explore unique ideas outside the constraints of his daily work.
